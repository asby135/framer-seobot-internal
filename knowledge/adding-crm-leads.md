Discover the 5 essential methods for building a robust prospect database on Telegram with CRMChat.

## How to Add Prospects to Your CRMChat Pipeline: 5 Methods Explained

Adding prospects, or leads, is the first step in building a successful sales pipeline. CRMChat offers several flexible methods to populate your pipeline, whether you're importing existing conversations, adding new contacts from networking events, or generating leads from online sources. This guide explains the five primary ways to add prospects to your pipeline.

### Method 1: Sync Telegram Folders

This is the fastest way to import all your existing business conversations into CRMChat at once. By syncing a Telegram folder, every contact within that folder is automatically added as a lead to your pipeline. You can access this feature by clicking the **My Leads** button.

Navigate to the main CRM view and click the **+** icon in the bottom right corner.

Select **Telegram Folder Sync** from the menu.

A list of your Telegram folders will appear. Toggle the switch next to the folder(s) you wish to sync.

Choose the correct workspace and click **Enable Sync**.

**Tip:** If you can't sync a folder, go to the folder settings and remove all **Chat Types** dynamic parameters such as **Contacts, Non-Contacts, Groups, Channels, Bots, Muted, Read, Archived**, and remove all folder** invite links**, if there are any. Then try to sync again, and the issue will be resolved.

### Method 2: Forward a Message

If you have an ongoing conversation with a potential prospect on Telegram, you can quickly add them as a lead by forwarding one of their messages to the CRMChat bot.

Open a chat with the person you want to add.

Right-click (or long-press on mobile) any message sent by them and select **Forward**.

In the pop-up, select the CRMChat bot as the recipient and send the message.

The bot will automatically create a new lead and save the forwarded message as a note in their profile.

### Method 3: Add Manually

For prospects you've met outside of Telegram, you can manually input their information to create a new lead.

From the main CRM view, click the **+** icon.

Select **Add Manually**.

Fill in the form with the prospect's information, including their **Name**, a short **Description**, and any contact details you have (URL, Email, Phone, or Telegram).

Click **Create lead** to save the prospect to your pipeline.

### Method 4: Scan a QR Code

This method is perfect for adding leads quickly during in-person meetings or conferences. Simply ask the prospect to show you their Telegram QR code.

On your mobile device, open the CRMChat mini-app and tap the **+** icon.

Select **Scan QR**.

Use your phone's camera to scan their QR code. The lead will be instantly added to your pipeline.

### Method 5: Import from External Sources

CRMChat allows you to generate new leads in bulk by parsing information from LinkedIn profiles.

**LinkedIn Profile Screenshot:** Take a screenshot of a prospect's LinkedIn profile and send the image directly to the CRMChat bot. Our AI will analyze the image, extract key information like their name and professional details, and create a new lead.

**Telegram Group Parser:** Go to the **Outreach** tab and select **Group Parser**. Enter the link of a public Telegram group to extract a complete list of its members, creating a new set of leads for your campaigns. You can also use our free parser tool on the website to find relevant groups based on keywords.

### Tips for Adding Prospects

**Use Folders for Speed:** Before you begin, organize your existing Telegram chats into folders (e.g., Clients, Hot Leads). This makes the **Telegram Folder Sync** method extremely efficient for importing dozens of contacts in seconds.

**Forward for Context:** When using the **Forward Message** method, choose a message that provides important context about the lead. This message is automatically saved as a note, giving you a quick reminder of your conversation history.

With these five methods, you can efficiently build and manage a robust prospect database directly within Telegram. Each method is designed to fit different workflows, from manual entry to automated bulk imports, ensuring your pipeline is always filled with high-quality leads.
