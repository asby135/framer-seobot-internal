### Can I pay in crypto?

Yes, we accept crypto payments, but only for quarterly plans. To proceed:

Start @crmchat_crm_bot and choose your plan

Provide your email address, where we'll send an invoice that you can pay with crypto

Every year on your email, you'll receive a new invoice for the next period. If the invoice is not paid within 2 weeks, your CRMChat access will be prohibited.

Message our support team to proceed with the crypto payment, and provide your email and the plan of choice. One of our team members will help you with this soon.

### Can I get my invoice?

You get invoices/receipts automatically when transactions are processed. Please check the email you registered with us and look in the Spam folder. If you need a manual invoice, message our support team , and we'll generate it for you through Stripe.
