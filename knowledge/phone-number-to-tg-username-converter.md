### What is the Phone Number to Telegram Username Converter? How does it work?

This tool allows you to convert phone numbers of your prospects into Telegram usernames, compatible with Telegram outreach sequences in CRMChat. Typically, 50% is the average enrichment rate for India, CIS, and MENA regions. 30% for EU, UK, Americas.

It's useful when you have the phone number of your prospect from Apollo/Clay, etc., but you are not able / don't want to call them.
