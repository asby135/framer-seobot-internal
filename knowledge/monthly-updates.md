# CRMChat Monthly Updates

## February 2026

### Flexible Pricing
New pricing model lets customers start with 1-2 Telegram accounts and add more as outreach grows, instead of committing to a fixed $499/mo plan for unlimited accounts. Pay for what you use, scale when you see results.

### Spintax Support and Message Preview
Sequences now support spintax variations — write `{Hey|Hi|Hello}` and each recipient gets a different version. More variation means fewer patterns for Telegram to flag, improving deliverability. New message preview shows exactly what recipients will see before launching, including random spintax picks.

## January 2026

### Automated Account Warmup
Fully automated warmup system that gradually builds account reputation, reducing restrictions and improving deliverability. Set it and forget it.

### Default Lead Owners with Round-Robin Assignment
Workspace admins can set default lead owners in sequence settings. Choose multiple team members and leads are automatically distributed using round-robin for balanced workloads.

### Outreach by Phone Numbers
Upload CSVs with phone numbers and CRMChat automatically finds associated Telegram accounts and sends messages. 2x higher deliverability compared to username-only outreach. Works with contacts from Apollo, Clay, and other prospecting tools.

### Detailed Campaign Analytics
View sent/read/replied message metrics for 7, 30, 90 days, or custom periods. Sales teams can track daily performance, managers get visibility into team conversation volumes.

## December 2025

### Tag Group Chat Members in Sequences and Use CRM Properties as Variables
Personalize sequences with CRM custom properties and tag specific members directly in group chat announcements.

### Smart Outreach Tips Built Into Campaign Builder
Proven recommendations embedded directly into the sequence builder, tested across hundreds of campaigns to help increase response rates and avoid account limitations.

## November 2025

### Deal Owner Notifications
CRM deal owners receive instant notifications when leads reply, regardless of which Telegram account they message. Never miss a hot lead response.

### Bulk Messaging for Group Chats
Broadcast updates across 100+ group chats simultaneously. The system automatically sends from the right account in each chat — no manual switching required.

### Bulk Edit Deals in the Pipeline View
Select multiple deals and update custom properties in one go — change stages, reassign owners, or modify any field across the pipeline with a few clicks.

### Telegram Group Parser Chrome Extension
Parse Telegram groups directly from the browser. Extract member lists from any public or private Telegram chat you're a part of.

## October 2025

Curated databases with 3,000+ verified Web3 professionals organized by industry and job title, complete with Telegram usernames.

### Lightning-Fast CRM Performance
CRM now handles thousands of leads without performance issues. Fixed a bug where notifications appeared for already-read conversations.

### Clay + CRMChat Integration
Bring email-level personalization to Telegram outreach. Use Clay and LinkedIn to enrich lead data, convert phone numbers to Telegram usernames, and craft hyper-personalized messages using variables like Company Name, Job Title, and more.

## September 2025

### Smart Duplicate Detection for Outreach Sequences
When uploading leads to a sequence, CRMChat shows warnings if there are duplicates across existing sequences, CRM deals, and connected Telegram accounts' chats. Keeps outreach clean and protects sender reputation.

### Advanced Lead Research Tools
Look-alike Audience Finder analyzes your existing audience to find similar people based on shared Telegram groups. Phone Number to Username Converter turns phone numbers from Apollo, Clay, etc. into Telegram usernames ready for outreach.

### Credit System for Lead Research Tools
Buy research credits upfront and get results delivered to Telegram. Group parsing is a flat 500 credits regardless of group size.

### Multiple Pipelines
Create separate pipelines for different workflows. Set up custom property filters, save the view, and get a dedicated pipeline. All pipelines show up in one dropdown.
