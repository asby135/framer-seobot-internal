# How to warm up Telegram Accounts for Outreach Campaigns

> 📖 Full article with screenshots: https://crmchat.ai/help-center/how-to-warm-up-telegram-accounts

## Introduction

Account warming is the process of gradually building activity and reputation for Telegram accounts before launching cold outreach campaigns. New or inactive accounts that suddenly start sending mass messages are flagged by Telegram's anti-spam systems, resulting in restrictions or permanent bans.

Proper account warming establishes your accounts as legitimate, active users—significantly reducing the risk of blocks and improving deliverability rates for your outreach campaigns.

**Time Required:** 10-14 days
**Recommended For:** All new outreach accounts and accounts with 2+ weeks of inactivity

## Why Account Warming Matters

**Without warming:**

Immediate account restrictions after first outreach

Reduced message delivery rates

Permanent bans require a new account purchase

**With proper warming:**

Natural activity patterns established

Sustainable campaigns lasting months

Instead of permanent bans, accounts get restricted for short periods of time.

## Account Requirements

### Which Accounts Need Warming:

Purchased accounts with no prior activity

Accounts inactive for 2+ weeks

### Set up Before Warming:

✅ Telegram Premium subscription for each account (required). Read here on how to send a Telegram Premium to all your accounts quickly.

✅ Complete profile: username, name, photo, bio

## Submit your accounts to warm up in the CRMChat Dashboard 

Go to **Outreach** → **Telegram Accounts **and connect your account to CRMChat. In the account setup panel, you'll find an account warmup button.

## Best Practices

### DO:

✅ Keep Telegram Premium active

### DON'T:

❌ Use automation tools during warming
❌ Send cold messages before completing warm-up

## Ongoing Maintenance

**Daily Limits (Post-Warming):**

Start: 5 cold messages/day

Increase by 2-3 every week

Maximum: 15 messages/account/day

## Troubleshooting

### Account Restricted After Warming

**Solution:**

Stop all outreach immediately

Contact @SpamBot from the restricted account, click /start

Pause campaigns for 1 day
