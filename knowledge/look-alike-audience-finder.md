# Look-Alike Audience Finder

> 📖 Full article with screenshots: https://crmchat.ai/help-center/look-alike-audience-finder

### What is the Look-alike audience finder? How does it work?

Let's say you uploaded a link to a group / a list of your current clients/accounts from your target audience. We look for the most popular mutual groups of these people, parse those groups, then repeat (if it's necessary to find more based on your request), and so on. From the source of 500 people, you'll definitely find more than 1,000 lookalikes.
