# Workspace Management & Team Collaboration

> 📖 Full article with screenshots: https://crmchat.ai/help-center/faq-workspace-management

### How do I delete a workspace?

To delete a workspace in CRMChat, follow these steps:

Open your CRMChat dashboard and navigate to your workspace settings

Go to **Settings** > **Invite team**

Delete everyone from the workspace (remove all team members)

Look for the specific workspace you wish to delete

Leave the workspace yourself

The workspace will be deleted automatically

**Important:** Always ensure you double-check the workspace you are deleting, as this action might be irreversible.

### How many workspaces can I add?

Five workspaces within your subscription.

### I cannot add a teammate

Ask your colleague to start the @crmchat_crm_bot first. The app will 'see' your colleague, and then you can invite. After that, the colleague needs to go to the @crmchat_crm_bot and accept the invite.

### I have added a teammate, but the link expired

No worries, revoke the invitation in the 'Invite Team' menu and send it again. Also, make sure that your colleague has started the app @crmchat_crm_bot, otherwise, the bot will not 'see' your colleague.
