# Telegram AI Sales Agent

> 📖 Full article with screenshots: https://crmchat.ai/help-center/telegram-ai-sales-agent

### How does the Telegram AI Agent work?

The bot will sit in your Telegram account(s) and reply to incoming messages. You can start the outreach using this account, and if a prospect replies, the bot will handle the conversation or wait for your intervention if the question is unclear.

According to the prompt, it will answer clients' questions and lead clients to perform actions like booking a meeting, following a link, or filling out a form.

### How do I set up the AI bot?

You will need 2 things:

A prompt

A knowledge base (needed if your product is complex and the bot needs FAQs)

To write the prompt, follow this link: https://chatgpt.com/g/g-685e295c9b948191b6ba84e7a03ff886-isales-ai-chatbot-instruction-assistant?model=gpt-4o
