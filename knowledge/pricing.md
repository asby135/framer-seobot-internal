# CRMChat Pricing Plans

CRMChat offers four plans: Starter (free), Personal, Team, and Agency. Both monthly and yearly billing are available — yearly billing saves 30%.

## Starter — Free forever

**Start closing deals faster with time-saving sales tools you can use for free**

No credit card required.

Includes:
- All core CRM Features
- 50 CRM leads
- Connect 1 Telegram Account
- CRM & Chat on one screen
- Telegram Folder Sync to CRM

Does NOT include:
- Unlimited CRM leads
- Access to 7,000+ integrations via Zapier
- Multiple workspaces
- Multiple TG accounts
- Team collaboration
- AI account warmup

**Comprehensive CRM and outreach tools built for collaborative sales teams**

Everything in Personal, plus:

Does NOT include:

### Outreach Account Pricing Tiers

The Team plan uses a tiered pricing model where the price per account decreases as your usage increases. When you reach each new tier threshold, additional accounts are billed at the lower rate.

| Accounts | Price per Account |
|----------|------------------|
| 1-3 accounts | $79/account |
| 4-6 accounts | $59/account |
| 7-9 accounts | $49/account |
| 10-12 accounts | $39/account |
| 13-15 accounts | $29/account |
| 16-18 accounts | $19/account |
| 19-20 accounts | $9/account |
| 21+ accounts | $5/account |

All prices shown are for monthly billing. Yearly billing receives a 30% discount on all tiers.

### Team Members

The first 3 team members are included at no additional cost. Each additional team member costs $10/month (monthly plan) or $7/month (yearly plan).

Need more than 50 accounts? Talk to Us.
