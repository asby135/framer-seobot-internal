# Outreach Issues

> 📖 Full article with screenshots: https://crmchat.ai/help-center/faq-outreach-issues

### Accounts do not send as many messages as I planned

This might be the reason:

Please add Telegram Premium to the accounts you use for outreach - it allows sending up to 15 cold messages daily

Check what exactly Failed message says (in Leads and Statistics) - put your mouse over the failed message to see the reason

Please go from each account to @SpamBot and click start - it will allow unblocking the account if it got blocked

Check if you have bought Telegram Premium for that account

Check daily limits (are they too high?)

Check the content of what you sent (are you customizing it?)

Check contact base (are you sending a relevant offer to relevant people?)

### Failed message says account was limited. What does it mean?

If the account was limited, go to @SpamBot and click start - it will allow you to unblock the account if it got blocked. To avoid the account being limited:

**If there are no issues like mentioned above, just restart the campaign by clicking Pause Sequence and start again.**
